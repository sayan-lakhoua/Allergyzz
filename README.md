<h2 align="center">Allergyzz</h2>

<p>
  Allergies affect over 1/3 of the population worldwide. Many of them are kids and young people, I’m one of them. 

However, when I was diagnosed, I did not receive any explanations about what an allergy is, an allergic reaction, an anaphylactic reaction, and more. I did not know where this was coming from… why me?

Allergyzz is an app designed for kids and young people to help them better understand and learn more about allergies. It’s inspired by the experience of a storybook, with the goal of creating something familiar to children while making it dynamic. Same as coding you learn by doing!

Allergyzz is an interactive story-driven experience designed to make learning about allergies engaging and accessible. You’ll meet Clearus, an immune cell, who will guide you through this story. In the app, you’ll be able to learn what allergies are and how they work, understand allergic reactions and anaphylaxis, practice using an epinephrine auto-injector, learn the right emergency steps to help save a life, discover when and how to call emergency services using your iPhone and more!   I designed and drew all the assets myself on iPad with my Apple Pencil. It was important to me to make the app feel personal, just like the story behind it. 
</p>
